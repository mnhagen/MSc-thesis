\section{Research Question(s)}

The research question investigated in this thesis is \ldots

Main research question:
\begin{itemize}
    \item To what extent can neural operator methods provide discretization-robust surrogate models for PDE-based data on both uniform grids and non-uniform point clouds?
\end{itemize}

Sub research questions:
\begin{itemize}
    \item To what extent do FNO and CNO generalize across grid resolutions on uniform-grid PDE benchmarks?
    \item How effectively can Geo-FNO extend Fourier neural operators to non-uniform point-cloud data?
    \item What limitations are observed when applying Geo-FNO to reactor-relevant non-uniform data?
\end{itemize}
